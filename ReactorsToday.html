Nuclear Reactors Today

Today (with the decommissioning of Indian Point Unit #2 in early 2020) there are 96 operating nuclear reactor units in the United States. These plants have as many as 3 reactor units, each of which can range from 500MWe to a huge 1.5GWe in net generating capacity.

Illinois has the most operating units of any state, with 11 units at 6 sites.
